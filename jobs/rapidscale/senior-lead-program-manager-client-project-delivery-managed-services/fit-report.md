**Tier:** Moderate  
**Verdict:** Reasonable fit with gaps - review gaps before applying

Richard brings 15+ years of cross-functional delivery on large, multi-year federal modernization programs, with repeated team leadership, PMO partnership, requirements and scope traceability, stakeholder coordination, training, and process-improvement work that maps to several program-management responsibilities in the JD. His Deloitte experience partnering with the PMO, CFO, Applications Development, and Business Modernization Office—and supporting planning, presentations, acceptance criteria, and defect resolution across complex technical dependencies—provides a credible consulting-to-delivery narrative. The main gaps are the absence of a formal Program or Project Manager title, no Professional Services or managed-services/cloud-MSP context, limited evidence of portfolio ownership, enterprise governance framework definition, ROI/business-case development, and explicit coaching of project managers. Apply only if he can position federal consulting program delivery and PMO-adjacent leadership as transferable to RapidScale’s client project delivery organization without overstating MSP, cloud infrastructure, or senior PM credentials.
